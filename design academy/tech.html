TECHNOLOGIES

  • 3D Printers

    The DREAM Lab has three 3D printers: two Lulzbot Tazs and a Lulzbot Mini. These tools allow students to produce 3 dimensional objects of their own design.

    learn more

  • X-Carve

    With the X-Carve CNC machine, students can carve a CAD (computed aided design) project into wood and plastics.

    learn more

  • Laser Cutter

    The Glowforge laser cutter allows students to cut and engrave dozens of materials from plywood to acrylic to even leather. They use their prefered software to create a design, then upload it to the Glowforge where it's printed with a laser beam the width of a strand of hair.

    learn more

  • Virtual Reality

    The Vive virtual reality system transports students into a fully immersive digital world. It lets them trael across the globe and experience unique educational simulations, all without leaving the lab.

    learn more

  • Embroidery

    The Brother SE1800 is an embroidery and sewing machine that transforms student-created digital designs into embroidery patterns and stitches them into fabric.

    learn more

  • Sewing

    The DREAM Lab has two Brother Project Runway computerized sewing machines. Students can use their 50 built in stitches and 87 stitch functions to create clothes and other fabric projects.

    learn more

  • Audio

    The DREAM Lab has several devices for creating audio recordings including multiple microphones and editing softwares.

  • Garden Wall

    The garden wall is a project undertaken by members of the Duchesne community with the goal of creating a self-sustaining ecosystem consisting of a plant wall and a fish tank. Currently, the wall portion is activly growing herbs for the lunch program, and its conditions are monitered by a Raspberry Pi.

  • Visual

    The DREAM Lab has many options for students to create visual productions including a padcaster and a 4K, cinema-quality camera. There is also access to high-end editing software such as Final Cut Pro.

  • Other

    The DREAM Lab also provides access to many other technologies. For physical creations, there is a Dremel and other tools. In addition, students can use the full Adobe suite and various CAD softwares. The lab is always adding new features, so check it out to see the most current technologies available.
