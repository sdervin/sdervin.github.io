ABOUT

In the fall of 2016, the DREAM (Duchesne Research Engineering Art Media) Lab opened to provide a space for students to explore STEAM and maker space principles. The lab is designed around those principles, which emphasize innovative and critical thinking, using technology to creatively solve problems, hands-on learning and experimenting, and collaboration and free thinking.

It is home to many different technologies, ranging from 3D printers to a cinema-quality camera, all of which students use to create unique projects.

In addition to hosting a variety of classes, the Lab is also generally open to all students in an effort to help prepare them for work and life in the 21st century.
